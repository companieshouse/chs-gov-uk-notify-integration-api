You must file your overdue confirmation and identity verification statements or make a representation. There are instructions on the back of this letter.

If you do not file the statements we may take enforcement action without further notice

This may include prosecution or financial penalties for the LLP and its memberscompany and its directors.

The LLP and its memberscompany and its directors may be committing an offence for failing to file a confirmation statement. Any memberdirector who continues to act as a memberdirector without verifying their identity is also committing an offence. The LLPcompany is committing an offence for failing to rectify this.

What you need to do

Step 1. Each memberdirector must verify their identity

For information on how to do this, search online for ‘Verifying your identity for Companies House’, or visit:
gov.uk/guidance/verifying-your-identity-for-companies-house

After each memberdirector has verified, they’ll receive a unique identifier called a Companies House personal code.

If you already have a personal code, you do not need to verify your identity again.

You’ll need each member’sdirector’s personal code for step 2.

Step 2. File the confirmation statement and verification statement

In the confirmation statement, you will need to provide the Companies House personal code for each memberdirector to submit their identity verification statements.

You need to do this even if a memberdirector has already provided their personal code and statement for other roles at this LLPcompany, or at other companies.

To file your confirmation statement, search GOV.UK for ‘file your confirmation statement with Companies House’ or visit:
gov.uk/file-your-confirmation-statement-with-companies-house

How to make representations

If there are exceptional circumstances you want us to consider before we proceed with enforcement action, you must write to us by [[${representation_due_date}]]. This is called a representation.

To make a representation, go to https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/make-a-representation-for-not-filing-an-identity-verification-statement

If we accept your representation, we will pause enforcement action for a limited period so you can file the statements. The LLP and its memberscompany and its directors will remain in default and we’ll resume enforcement if you do not successfully file the statements.
