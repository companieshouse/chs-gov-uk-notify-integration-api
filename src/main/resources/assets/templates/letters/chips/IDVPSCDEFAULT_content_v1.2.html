Overdue identity verification statement for your role as a person with significant control (PSC) of [[${company_name}]]




You have not delivered a statement to Companies House to confirm your identity.
This was due by [[${verification_due_date}]].

This is a new legal requirement under the Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2023. All limited liability partnership (LLP) memberscompany directors and PSCs must verify their identity and submit a statement to Companies House for each role in each LLPcompany.

Your deadline has passed and cannot be extended because it is a legal requirement to verify your identity.

You must submit your identity verification statement or make a representation by [[${representation_due_date}]]. There are instructions on the back of this letter.

If you do not file the statement you may be committing an offence.
You may face prosecution or a financial penalty.

What you need to do

1. Verify your identity

For information on how to do this, search online for ‘Verifying your identity for Companies House’, or visit: gov.uk/guidance/verifying-your-identity-for-companies-house

After you’ve completed your identity verification check you’ll get a unique identifier, called a Companies House personal code.

If you already have a personal code, you do not need to verify your identity again.

You’ll need your personal code for step 2.

2. Submit your identity verification statement to Companies House

Search online for the ‘Provide identity verification details for a person with significant control’ service, or visit:
gov.uk/guidance/provide-psc-verification

Provide your personal code. This is so we can connect your verified identity to the PSC name on our records. You’ll also be asked to tick a statement confirming that you’ve verified your identity.

You need to do this even if you’ve already done it for other roles you have at this LLPcompany, or at other companies.

How to make representations

If there are exceptional circumstances you want us to consider before we proceed with enforcement action, you must write to us by [[${action_due_date}]]. This is called a representation.

To make a representation, go to https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/make-a-representation-for-not-filing-an-identity-verification-statement.

If we accept your representation, we will pause enforcement action for a limited period so you can file the statement. You will remain in default and enforcement action will resume if you do not submit the statement.

Check the register

If you are no longer a PSC of the LLPcompany or your details on the register need to be corrected, the LLPcompany must submit a filing to update its record.
